===Debian-Packages, JDK7, Bug fixes

Date: 27.11.2011

A new release (version 0.50-1) of jGameBase has been released.

<br><b>New Functionality</b>
&nbsp;&nbsp;- <i>Release a debian package</i>
&nbsp;&nbsp;- <i>Write messages to a log file</i>
&nbsp;&nbsp;- <i>Made jGameBase jdk7 compatible</i>
&nbsp;&nbsp;- <i>Splash screen and main window show version</i>
&nbsp;&nbsp;- <i>Preliminary support for MS Windows</i>

<br><b>Bugs Fixed</b>
&nbsp;&nbsp;- <i>Fixed a problem loading bmp images</i>
&nbsp;&nbsp;- <i>Database icon and splash can be image type other than gif</i>
&nbsp;&nbsp;- <i>Database icon and splash will be searched for if not found</i>
