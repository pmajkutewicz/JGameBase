===First Release Candidate Released

Date: 26.09.2009

The first release candidate (version 0.44) of jGameBase has been released.

<br><b>Bugs Fixed</b>
&nbsp;&nbsp;- <i>Database hang fixed</i>

<br><b>New Features</b>
&nbsp;&nbsp;- <i>The jToolbox is no longer needed</i>

&nbsp;&nbsp;- <i>File System Hierarchy Standard (FHS)</i>

&nbsp;&nbsp;- <i>You can now specify multiple paths for all objects</i>

&nbsp;&nbsp;- <i>Default paths</i>

&nbsp;&nbsp;- <i>Support for RAR and 7Zip compressed files.</i>
